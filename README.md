# Employee Survey Analysis Capstone Project

## Overview
This project analyzes an employee engagement survey conducted by Pierce County WA. The dataset contains 14,725 responses and 10 fields. The goal is to identify employee satisfaction trends, highlight departmental patterns, and provide insights to improve engagement.

## Dataset Description
- **Number of Records:** 14,725
- **Fields:** 10
- **Key Fields:** Question, Response, Department, Role

## Questions Answered
1. Which survey questions did respondents agree with or disagree with most?  
2. Do you see any patterns or trends by department or role?  
3. As an employer, what steps might you take to improve employee satisfaction based on the survey results?

## Key Insights
- Highest rated statements: “I know what is expected of me at work” (3.48), “Overall I am satisfied with my job” (3.30), etc.  
- Lowest rated statements: “I have a best friend at work” (2.60), “Opportunities to learn and grow” (~2.80)  
- Departmental trends: Emergency Management (3.37) and Family Justice Center (3.51) scored highest; Sheriff’s Department (2.39) lowest.  
- Overall average score: 2.98 — moderately positive but room for improvement.

## Tools Used
- Excel (Pivot Tables) for analysis  
- Power BI for visualization
